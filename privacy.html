4. Special Category Data

Where learners voluntarily disclose information relating to health conditions or neurodivergence (e.g. ADHD, autism) when selecting specialism filters, this is special category data under UK GDPR Article 9. We process this only on the basis of your explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a)) and solely to match you with an appropriate instructor. You are not required to provide this information.

We share your data only as necessary: with instructors you contact, with Stripe for payment processing (stripe.com/gb/privacy), with our email provider to send transactional messages, and with analytics providers (subject to cookie consent). We do not sell your data.

Under UK GDPR you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, request erasure, restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at privacy@passd-ai.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month.

You have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint or 0303 123 1113. We'd appreciate the chance to address concerns first — please email us at privacy@passd-ai.co.uk.
