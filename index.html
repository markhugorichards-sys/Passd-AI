The Blocker

One restriction.
One certificate.
Transaction complete.

A Form LL restriction sits on your title at Land Registry. It doesn't go away on its own — but it doesn't have to stop your transaction either. The moment a specialist solicitor verifies your identity and issues the Certificate of Compliance, the restriction is satisfied and your sale or remortgage can proceed.

That certificate is all you need. FormLL gets it into your inbox — signed, valid, ready to hand to your transaction solicitor.

The Restriction Wording
"No disposition of the registered estate by the proprietor of the registered estate is to be registered without a certificate signed by a conveyancer that the conveyancer is satisfied that the person who executed the document submitted for registration as disponor is the same person as the proprietor."
In plain English: you cannot sell or remortgage without an independent solicitor confirming in writing that the people signing are the actual registered owners.
